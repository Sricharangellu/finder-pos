## Pre-existing bug found and fixed

`InventoryService.adjustStock` INSERT referenced an `id` column that does not exist on
`inventory_stock` (composite PK `tenant_id, location_id, product_id`). Every first
adjustment at a location for a product would 500. Unit tests never caught it (stubbed DB);
found by the live-Postgres probe below. Fixed by removing the phantom column from the INSERT.
